What Mora Solves

Restaurants lose customers every day because phones ring during rushes. Staff get overwhelmed, callers hang up, and orders get rushed or misheard.

Mora removes the chaos by handling every call instantly — taking orders, checking hours, answering menu questions, and passing accurate tickets straight to your POS.

Why Restaurants Choose Mora

  • Never miss a call — Mora answers instantly 24/7.
  • Reduces staff stress during peak hours.
  • Takes accurate, itemized orders with sizes & modifiers.
  • Integrates directly with Square (others coming soon).
  • Sounds warm, friendly, and natural — not robotic.

How Mora Works

  1. Customer calls your restaurant number.
  2. Mora answers instantly with your restaurant’s personality.
  3. Mora takes orders accurately — sizes, modifiers, everything.
  4. Orders go directly into your POS.
  5. Your team focuses on cooking, not phones.
